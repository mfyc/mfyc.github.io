About Me

My name is Monica Chiu and I'm a junior at Boston University majoring in Computer Science. I am currently 21 years old despite my rather old-looking visage.
I am an avid tv consumer! I generally focus on American tv shows and Asian dramas. Within Asian dramas, I am particularly fond of Korean dramas so when I'm not waiting for the next season of Black Mirror or Sherlock, you can bet I'm watching some over-the-top, hilarious Korean hijinks. Aside from the dramas though, I am an absolute fan of Korean and Japanese culture in general. Once I start, I can talk about anything and everything about Korean and Japanese culture for days.
Besides watching tv/variety shows, I split the majority of the rest of my time either gaming or working on my academics. I am a PC gamer through and through, though I also dabble in mobile games here and there. My favorite genre of games ranges from vast, open-world sandbox games to indie survival games. My all-time favorites are Elder Scroll Series, Minecraft(back in the day), XCOM, Fallout 3, MassEffect 1,2, etc. Some indie games that I've had an absolute blast playing (off the top of my head) are Stardew Valley, Rimworld, Undertale, Papers Please, Prison Architect, etc.

Now, what do I want to achieve as a student majoring in CS? The truth is, I'm not quite sure yet. I have several ideas about what directions I could take. Game development for one or I could go into web development and focus more on the front-end side or I could become a master Data Scientist. There's just too many cool things I can do/get into as a CS major so it's both exhilarating and overwhelming. The only thing I am definitely sure about is that I want to try anything and everything!
